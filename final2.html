Building your PC

You finally made it this far! After snagging the hottest deals on multiple parts, making sure that all of your parts are compatible and, picking your graphics card after many days of contemplation, you finally get to build it! After your parts come in you want to unbox all of them, and then set them down gently on a surface that doesnt conduct electricty (plastic/wood table), so that your parts dont get a short circuit and end up DOA (especially with motherboards). After that I recommend watching this video by Austin Evans to guide you through building it as a visual aid is much more useful than me explaining it.

Installing windows and setting up:

You're now so close to finally starting your amazing gaming
experiance on PC. However, after getting your PC to boot,
you need to immediatly get some things out of the way. These
things include installing windows, and updating drivers. To
do these somewhat challenging tasks I recommend watching
this video by paul's hardware. The only things you'll need
to download windows are a USB 3 flash drive and a windows 10
license. You can buy a license from pretty much any big box
retailer or online, however I personally recommend using Kinguin
as they sell legit license for only $30. However some people have
had complaints with this website so if you want to make sure
you don't get scammed, you can buy a lisence from Micrsoft
however it is very pricy. After downloading windows you can
finally download your favorite game launcher (steam,origin etc.)
and game away!
