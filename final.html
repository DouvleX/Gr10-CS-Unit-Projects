How to build a gaming computer

Welcome to the ultimate gaming computer building guide. This guide will walk you through determining your budget, picking your parts (depending on your use), and assembling the build!

Building a gaming PC can be scary and daunting, however after
finishing this guide you will become an expert gaming computer
builder. There are many advantages of building a gaming computer
rather than using a console such as, tons of exclusives,
cheaper games, and no fee for online services etc. The first
step to building any gaming rig is deciding what your budget is
going to be (how much youre willing to spend). This varies depending
on what games you're planning to play, for example, someone who is
only going to play competive esport type games such as CS:GO might
not need a graphics card or might want to buy a cheap one. However
someone who is planning on playing graphically intensive games
such as the Witcher 3 might want to buy an expensive high-end
graphics card. While determining your budget, you might want to
consider the cost of other items such as the price of a monitor,
windows, keyboard and mouse , and any other accessories you may
need, as these might take a large portion of your budget. Once
you're done detrmining your budget, you can move on to picking your
parts!
